Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Florida Food Festival ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website floridafoodfestival.com (the "Site").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors to our Site except as described below:

Analytics Information

We use Google Analytics to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. This helps us track the number of visitors to the various pages on our Site, understand how visitors use our Site, and improve our Site accordingly. This information is processed in a way that does not identify anyone. We do not make any attempt to find out the identities of those visiting our Site through Google Analytics.

Cookies

Our Site uses cookies, which are small text files placed on your device to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. These cookies allow us to distinguish you from other users of the Site, which helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our Site and also allows us to improve our Site.

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this Site may become inaccessible or not function properly.

Third-Party Services

Advertising

We may display advertisements on our Site. These advertisements may be delivered by third-party advertising partners, who may use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies to collect information about your activities on our Site and other websites. This information allows them to deliver targeted advertisements that may be of interest to you. This Privacy Policy does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Affiliate Links

Our Site may contain affiliate links to products or services offered by third parties. If you click on an affiliate link and subsequently make a purchase, we may receive a small commission. This does not result in any additional charge to you. These affiliate links help support our Site and allow us to continue to provide valuable content.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of each website you visit.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect through Google Analytics to:

  • Analyze trends and track visitor movements on the Site
  • Gather demographic information about our user base as a whole
  • Improve our Site and enhance user experience

Data Retention

We retain analytics data according to Google Analytics' data retention policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request that we delete any personal information we hold about you
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not provide any information on this Site.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.

Disclaimer

Please note that we are not affiliated with any of the festivals or events listed on our Site. We provide information about these events as a service to our users, but we are not responsible for the conduct or policies of these events or their organizers.
